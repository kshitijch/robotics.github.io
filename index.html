ABOUT US

The Robotics Club at the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Bhopal is a student-run organization focused on promoting the development and application of robotics technology. The club is comprised of enthusiastic and passionate students who share a common interest in robotics and technology. They work together to design and build various kinds of robots for a wide range of applications, including competition, research, and practical use. The club also hosts events, workshops, and competitions to enhance the skills and knowledge of its members and to promote the field of robotics to the wider community. With access to cutting-edge technology and experienced mentors, the Robotics Club at NIT Bhopal is a great platform for students to develop their skills, learn new technologies and contribute to the advancement of robotics.
